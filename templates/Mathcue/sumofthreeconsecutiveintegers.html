What are Three Consecutive Integers?

Integers are those numbers that can be shown without a fractional part. Basically, they are the whole number including both positive and negative terms and zero. For example, you can say that 8/4 is also an integer as it can easily be represented in whole numbers as 2.

The numbers which follow up with each other in a sequence or in order are called consecutive integers such as their difference is fixed. The three consecutive integers can be represented by

x, x+2, and x+4.

Solved Examples on Three Consecutive Integers Calculator

Example:

The three consecutive integers have a sum of 51. Find the three consecutive integers.

Solution:

Given input is 3 consecutive integers = 51

Thus, we know that sum of the three consecutive integers is 51.

Let the first integer be x, then the other two integers are x + 1 and x + 2.

According to the question, x + (x + 1) + (x + 2) = 51

⇒ x + x + 1 + x + 2 = 51

⇒ 3x + 3 = 51

⇒ 3(x + 1) = 51

⇒ x+1 = 51 / 3

⇒ x+1 = 17 (because, 3*17 = 51)

⇒ x = 17 - 1

⇒ x = 16

⇒ x + 2 = 17 + 2 = 18

Hence, the result of 3 consecutive integers whose sum is 51 is 16,17,18.
