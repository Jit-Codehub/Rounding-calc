What are Factor Pairs?

Factor pair is defined as a set of two factors that when multiplied together gives the number. Or factor pair is a set of 2 numbers whose product is the original number. A number can have multiple factor pairs.

Example: 42 is the product of 6 and 7. So (6, 7) is the factor pair of 42.

Steps to Compute Factor Pairs

The detailed steps to calculate the factor pairs of a number are along the lines:

  • Consider a number to get the factor pairs.
  • Find all the factors of the number.
  • List out the factor pairs whose product gives the original number.

Example:

Find the factor pairs of 24.

Solution:

Given number is 24

Factors of 24 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24

We can write 24 as a product of 1x24, 2x12, 3x8, 4x6

So, the factor pairs of 24 are (1, 24), (2, 12), (3, 8), (4, 6)
